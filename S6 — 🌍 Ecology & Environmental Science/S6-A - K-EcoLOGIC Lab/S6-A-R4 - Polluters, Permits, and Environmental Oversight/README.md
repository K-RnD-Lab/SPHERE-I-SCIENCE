# S6-A-R4 - Polluters, Permits, and Environmental Oversight

`S6-A-R4` is the fourth research program under `K-EcoLOGIC Lab`.

Its purpose is to connect oversight-oriented environmental records into a practical analytical layer for:

- permits and EIA visibility
- industrial pressure mapping
- compliance and monitoring context
- activist and researcher evidence workflows

## Why this belongs in S6

The program still studies environmental systems through:

- industrial environmental pressure
- regulatory traces tied to pollution and risk
- oversight data connected to ecological outcomes

That keeps it within `S6 - Ecology & Environmental Science`.
