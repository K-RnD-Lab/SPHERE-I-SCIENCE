# S6-A-R3 - Water Watch Ukraine

`S6-A-R3` is the third research program under `K-EcoLOGIC Lab`.

Its purpose is to build a structured Ukrainian water-observability layer for:

- surface-water monitoring
- quality and contamination trend interpretation
- basin and oblast level comparisons
- public-interest reporting and evidence support

## Why this belongs in S6

The research unit is an environmental system centered on:

- rivers and surface waters
- regional water quality
- contamination indicators
- ecological monitoring context

That makes it a strong fit for `S6 - Ecology & Environmental Science`.
