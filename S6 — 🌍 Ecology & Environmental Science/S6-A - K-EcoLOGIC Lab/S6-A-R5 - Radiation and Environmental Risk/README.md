# S6-A-R5 - Radiation and Environmental Risk

`S6-A-R5` is the fifth research program under `K-EcoLOGIC Lab`.

Its purpose is to connect radiation-monitoring context into a practical analytical layer for:

- monitoring-network visibility
- regional anomaly awareness
- source and platform coverage interpretation
- public-interest risk communication

## Live Surface

- public platform

- module page
  - `Radiation & Risk`

Other modules on the same platform:

- `SortSmart Ukraine`
- `Air & Exposure`
- `Water Watch`
- `Polluters & Permits`

## Why this belongs in S6

The program studies environmental systems through:

- radiation background context
- monitoring-network geography
- regional environmental risk communication
- links to broader pollution and emergency narratives

That keeps it within `S6 - Ecology & Environmental Science`.
