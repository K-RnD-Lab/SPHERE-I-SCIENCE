# S6 Ecology & Environmental Science

`S6` covers environmental systems rather than organism-intrinsic molecular biology.

## What belongs here

- ecosystems and biodiversity patterns
- environmental communities and microbiomes
- climate-linked system effects
- pollution, stress, and ecological network responses

## Example directions

- environmental microbiome sentinels
- biodiversity stress markers
- pollutant-response networks

## Boundary rule

Use `S6` when the research unit is an ecosystem, environmental community, or ecological system. Keep plant-intrinsic questions in `S2` and agricultural intervention questions in `S3`.
