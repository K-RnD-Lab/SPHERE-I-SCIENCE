# S3 Agricultural Biology & Biofertilizers

`S3` covers applied agro-biology and intervention logic around crop systems.

## What belongs here

- rhizosphere and soil microbiome questions
- biofertilizer design and efficacy logic
- crop-growth systems and intervention effects
- soil-health and agricultural support modeling

## Example directions

- rhizosphere microbiome scoring
- biofertilizer efficacy modeling
- soil-health predictors

## Boundary rule

Use `S3` when the work is about field-facing or intervention-facing agricultural systems. Keep plant-intrinsic phytochemical and metabolite questions in `S2`.
