# 🌿 S2 — Green / Sustainability

## Focus
Plant science, phytochemistry, green chemistry, and sustainable bioprocesses.

## Active Research Lines
- **Phytochemistry** — bioactive compound extraction and characterization
- **Green chemistry** — solvent-free synthesis, catalytic methods
- **Sustainable agriculture** — biofertilizers, soil microbiome
- **Environmental monitoring** — biosensors, ecological indicators

## Starter Tasks
1. Survey bioactive compounds in local flora
2. Design a green extraction protocol
3. Map soil microbiome diversity
4. Build a sustainability scorecard
