# 🩺 S1 — HealthTech / Biomedical & Oncology

## Focus
Biomedical research, oncology biomarkers, nucleic-acid therapeutics, clinical AI, and molecular diagnostics.

## Active Research Lines
- **BRCA2 miRNA** — biomarker discovery for hereditary cancer
- **Nucleic-acid therapeutics** — antisense, siRNA, and aptamer platforms
- **Bioinformatics & AI** — ML pipelines for clinical decision support
- **Molecular diagnostics** — liquid biopsy, companion diagnostics

## Tools & Packages
- [bioscore](https://pypi.org/project/bioscore/) — reproducibility audit, data quality, model readiness
- [Cancer Research Suite](https://huggingface.co/spaces/K-RnD-Lab/Cancer-Research-Suite_03-2026) — interactive HF Space

## Starter Tasks
1. Run `bioscore.reproducibility()` on your latest notebook
2. Classify your project with `set_method.classify()`
3. Register your study with `studyreg.register()`
4. Explore TCGA-BRCA dataset in the Cancer Research Suite
