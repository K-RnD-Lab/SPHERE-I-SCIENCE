# S2 Plant Science & Phytochemistry

`S2` covers plant-intrinsic biology rather than agricultural intervention systems.

## What belongs here

- phytochemicals and plant-derived bioactive compounds
- plant metabolites and pathway-level signatures
- plant molecular traits and stress-response biology
- compound-target logic tied to plant systems

## Example directions

- phytochemical atlas
- compound-target prioritization
- plant stress-response signatures

## Boundary rule

Use `S2` when the main question is about the plant itself. Move soil, rhizosphere, crop-support, and biofertilizer intervention questions to `S3`.
