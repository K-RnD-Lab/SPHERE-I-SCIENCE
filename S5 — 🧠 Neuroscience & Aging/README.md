# S5 Neuroscience & Aging

`S5` covers brain, cognition, neuroinflammation, and computational aging questions.

## What belongs here

- neurodegeneration and aging signals
- cognition and longitudinal vulnerability patterns
- neuroinflammation and biomarker-facing brain questions
- computational models of neurological risk or resilience

## Example directions

- neuroinflammation biomarker panels
- cognitive aging predictors
- vulnerability scoring

## Boundary rule

Use `S5` when the central research object is the nervous system, cognition, or aging-related brain function. Cross-organism metabolomic methods can still draw on `S4`, but the domain home stays in `S5`.
