# 📚 S7 — K Life OS

Science-facing lane for measurable life systems, cognition, adaptive training, self-tracking, and longitudinal human-pattern research.

This direction belongs to **SPHERE I — SCIENCE** inside K R&D Lab.
It is the natural scientific home for:
- long-horizon preparation research
- structured learning systems
- cognition-linked progress tracking
- adaptive training systems
- selected measurable life-pattern studies over time

---

## Why S7 Exists

Not every research line in science has to be wet-lab or disease-centered.
Some lines are about how people learn, adapt, structure their lives, build cognitive endurance, and improve under measurable systems.

That is why `S7` exists.

It gives a scientific home to projects where the object of study is:
- learning as a system
- cognition as a process
- training as an adaptive loop
- life structure as measurable pattern over time
- progress as evidence rather than impression only
