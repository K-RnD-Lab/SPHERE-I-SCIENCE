# S2-C-R2 Source Registry

## Research home

- Sphere: `S2`
- Lane: `S2-C Stress Biology & Resilience`
- Research line: `S2-C-R2 Plant Resilience And Recovery Profiles`

## Purpose

This registry tracks the first primary sources used to build a recovery-phase plant resilience panel.

The aim is to move beyond stress-response detection and toward:

- recovery capacity
- rewatering trajectories
- resilience-linked marker prioritization

## Current active scope

For the first execution pass, `S2-C-R2` is narrowed to:

- drought and rewatering contexts
- physiological and transcriptomic recovery
- genotype-aware resilience interpretation where available

## Current stance

At this stage, `S2-C-R2` should be treated as:

- a recovery-profile panel

not as:

- a universal drought-resilience model
- a crop deployment protocol

## What to collect next

The next useful sources should include:

- one stronger root-recovery study
- one metabolite-first recovery paper
- one study with explicit resilient vs vulnerable genotype comparison

## Claim discipline

From this registry, we may claim:

- that specific traits and signatures are associated with recovery after drought stress
- that recovery can be incomplete, delayed, or genotype-dependent
- that rewatering creates its own interpretable biological state

We should not yet claim:

- stable cross-species thresholds for resilience
- field-level recovery prediction from a single marker
- universal rewatering rules
