# S2-D-R1 Source Registry

## Research home

- Sphere: `S2`
- Lane: `S2-D Plant-Pathogen & Defense Systems`
- Research line: `S2-D-R1 Core Plant Defense Signature Panel`

## Purpose

This registry tracks the primary sources used to build the first defense-signature panel for `S2-D-R1`.

The aim is not to collect every plant immunity paper.

The aim is to identify a small set of signatures that recur across distinct plant-pathogen systems and are strong enough to anchor future research.

## Current active scope

For the first execution pass, `S2-D-R1` is narrowed to:

- bacterial and fungal resistance contexts
- hormone and early-response signatures
- defense-metabolite and pathway signals

## Current stance

At this stage, `S2-D-R1` should be treated as:

- a cross-system defense-signature panel

not as:

- a complete immunity ontology
- a validated crop protection recipe

## What to collect next

The next useful sources should include:

- one strong chitin / PRR-triggered immunity study
- one strong phenylpropanoid defense metabolite case
- one host-resistance marker comparison across resistant vs susceptible genotypes

## Claim discipline

From this registry, we may claim:

- that a signature repeatedly appears as a defense-linked signal
- that a signal is supported in bacterial or fungal resistance contexts
- that the signature is strong enough to enter a compact interpretation panel

We should not yet claim:

- field-level effectiveness
- universal cross-species equivalence
- direct agricultural intervention value
