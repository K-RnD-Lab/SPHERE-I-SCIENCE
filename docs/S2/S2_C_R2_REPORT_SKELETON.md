# S2-C-R2 Report Skeleton

## Title

Plant Resilience And Recovery Profiles

## Research home

- Sphere: `S2`
- Lane: `S2-C Stress Biology & Resilience`
- Research line: `S2-C-R2`

## Research question

Which signatures best distinguish plants that recover after drought from plants that only survive drought?

## Scope

This first report should stay narrow:

- drought and rewatering contexts
- 5-7 recovery signatures
- physiological plus molecular layers

## Data included

- plant system
- stress context
- recovery signature
- signature class
- recovery layer
- mechanism note
- evidence level

## Methods

### Source collection

- recent primary open-access studies
- at least one transcriptomic recovery study
- at least one physiology-first recovery study

### Standardization

- normalize recovery-signature names
- separate molecular, physiological, and scoring layers
- assign evidence level and priority score

### Prioritization logic

Signature ranking should combine:

- clarity of recovery relevance
- evidence strength
- cross-system usefulness
- complementarity across layers

## Outputs

- recovery-signature evidence table
- compact resilience shortlist
- one stress-vs-recovery comparison summary
- one figure for recovery layers

## Limits

- drought systems are not identical across species
- recovery timing differs strongly
- rewatering studies are methodologically heterogeneous

## Conclusion

The first report should end with:

- which markers best indicate recovery capacity
- which markers reflect incomplete restoration
- what next evidence is needed to strengthen the panel
