# S5-D-R2 Research Guide

## Research home

- Sphere: `S5`
- Lane: `S5-D Brain Resilience & Recovery`
- Research line: `S5-D-R2 Recovery Pattern Interpretation`

## Why this study belongs here

`S5-D-R2` extends resilience into recovery. It focuses on post-injury, post-stroke, and rehabilitation-facing brain recovery patterns without turning the sphere into general rehab medicine.

## Core question

Which biomarkers, network signals, reserve factors, and interventions help interpret recovery patterns after brain injury or vascular brain events?

## Practical substudies

### `S5-D-R2a` Recovery-Phase Signal Signatures

Goal:

- identify signals associated with recovery after stroke, TBI, or acquired brain injury

Focus:

- fluid biomarkers
- neuroimaging and network connectivity
- cognitive recovery endpoints
- mobility or function when relevant

### `S5-D-R2b` Resilience-Vs-Recovery Pattern Comparison

Goal:

- separate pre-existing reserve from post-event recovery processes

Focus:

- cognitive reserve in recovery
- rehabilitation responsiveness
- post-stroke cognitive recovery
- chronic injury biomarker context

## Claim discipline

At this stage we may say:

- recovery patterns can be studied through reserve, biomarkers, imaging, and intervention response
- cognitive reserve may influence recovery after stroke or TBI
- post-event recovery and pre-event resilience should be separated

At this stage we should not say:

- that recovery markers guarantee outcome
- that rehabilitation response is predictable from one marker
- that TBI, stroke, and neurodegeneration recovery are the same biological process
