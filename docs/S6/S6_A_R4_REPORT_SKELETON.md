# S6-A-R4 Report Skeleton

## Working title

Polluters, Permits, and Environmental Oversight: Open Permit Signals For Public-Interest Review

## Research home

- Sphere: `S6`
- Lane: `S6-A K-EcoLOGIC Lab`
- Research line: `S6-A-R4 Polluters, Permits, and Environmental Oversight`

## Question

What can open permit data show as a public-interest environmental oversight signal?

## Scope

Include:

- permit record summaries
- city and entity context
- air-permit overlap where available
- coverage and source limitations

Exclude:

- violation claims
- enforcement conclusions
- causal pollution attribution

## Methods

1. normalize permit records
2. summarize by city or entity
3. connect with air context where available
4. label coverage gaps
5. write follow-up questions rather than accusations

## Outputs

- evidence table
- permit summary
- air-permit crosswalk note
- public-interest caveat section

## Limits

- current permit module is a pilot coverage layer
- permit presence is not violation
- crosswalk overlap is contextual

## Conclusion

`S6-A-R4` should create a responsible oversight context layer that helps people know what to review next.
