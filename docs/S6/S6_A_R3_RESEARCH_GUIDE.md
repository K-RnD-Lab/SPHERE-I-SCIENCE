# S6-A-R3 Research Guide

## Research home

- Sphere: `S6`
- Lane: `S6-A K-EcoLOGIC Lab`
- Research line: `S6-A-R3 Water Watch Ukraine`

## Why this study belongs here

`S6-A-R3` is the water-monitoring layer of `K-EcoLOGIC Lab`.

It should turn official surface-water monitoring observations into basin-level environmental context without pretending to replace official hydrological assessment.

## Core question

How can open surface-water monitoring data be summarized into basin-level signals, coverage notes, and public-interest water-watch interpretation?

## Practical substudies

### `S6-A-R3a` Basin-Level Monitoring Summaries

Goal:

- summarize observation counts and indicator availability by basin or monitoring unit

Focus:

- river basin
- monitoring post
- observation count
- indicator availability
- latest observation period

### `S6-A-R3b` Water-Quality Coverage And Gap Notes

Goal:

- make data coverage visible before drawing interpretation

Focus:

- missing observations
- uneven basin coverage
- indicator gaps
- source-update limits

## Required outputs

- water-watch evidence table
- source registry
- basin summary report skeleton
- preliminary findings note

## Claim discipline

At this stage we may say:

- official water-monitoring data can support basin-level public interpretation
- coverage and indicator availability are themselves useful signals
- Water Watch is a monitoring-context module

At this stage we should not say:

- that the module replaces official water-quality assessment
- that one observation proves basin-level condition
- that missing data means absence of risk
