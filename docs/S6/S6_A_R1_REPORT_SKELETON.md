# S6-A-R1 Report Skeleton

## Working title

SortSmart Ukraine: Regional Waste, Sorting Readiness, and Recovery Potential

## Research home

- Sphere: `S6`
- Lane: `S6-A K-EcoLOGIC Lab`
- Research line: `S6-A-R1 SortSmart Ukraine`

## Question

Where does open Ukrainian waste data show the clearest sorting-readiness gaps and recovery opportunities?

## Scope

Include:

- regional waste outcomes
- waste-management facility registry signals
- material-recovery assumptions
- modeled avoided CO2e potential
- current public platform outputs

Exclude:

- real-time waste tracking
- direct facility-capacity validation unless a source exists
- regulatory enforcement claims

## Methods

1. normalize official waste and facility datasets
2. join regional outcomes with facility signals
3. calculate readiness and recovery indicators
4. apply transparent material-factor assumptions
5. produce regional comparison tables and figures

## Outputs

- evidence table
- regional ranking
- readiness interpretation note
- assumption and limitation section
- figure pack later

## Figures

- regional sorting-readiness ranking
- recovery vs landfill-disposal comparison
- facility-count context chart
- modeled avoided CO2e scenario chart

## Limits

- avoided CO2e is modeled, not directly measured
- registry presence does not prove facility operational status
- official data may have reporting lag

## Conclusion

`S6-A-R1` should show how open environmental data can become a reproducible sorting-readiness and recovery-potential research layer for Ukraine.
