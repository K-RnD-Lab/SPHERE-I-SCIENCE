# S6-A-R4 Research Guide

## Research home

- Sphere: `S6`
- Lane: `S6-A K-EcoLOGIC Lab`
- Research line: `S6-A-R4 Polluters, Permits, and Environmental Oversight`

## Why this study belongs here

`S6-A-R4` is the oversight and public-accountability layer of `K-EcoLOGIC Lab`.

It should organize permits data into reviewable signals without claiming enforcement authority.

## Core question

How can open emissions-permit data be translated into entity, city, and coverage signals that support public-interest environmental review?

## Practical substudies

### `S6-A-R4a` Entity-Level Oversight And Permit Tracking

Goal:

- summarize permit records by entity, city, and administrative context

Focus:

- permit holder
- city or region
- source record count
- pollutant or activity context where available

### `S6-A-R4b` Public-Interest Flagging Logic

Goal:

- create transparent context flags for follow-up rather than accusations

Focus:

- concentration of records
- overlap with air context
- missing or partial fields
- source coverage limits

## Required outputs

- permits evidence table
- source registry
- report skeleton
- preliminary findings note

## Claim discipline

At this stage we may say:

- permits data can support oversight context
- record concentration and crosswalks can guide follow-up
- partial coverage must be explicit

At this stage we should not say:

- that permit records prove violation
- that crosswalk overlap proves pollution impact
- that this module replaces official enforcement
