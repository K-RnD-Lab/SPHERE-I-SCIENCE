# S6-A To S6-E Research Map

## Purpose

This document turns `S6-A ... S6-E` into a practical lane-level map.

The goal is to keep:

- the lane stable
- two main research directions inside each lane
- one to two substudies inside each research direction

`S6` should stay ecology- and environment-facing, with clear public-interest relevance where appropriate.

## Structural rule

Use:

- `S6-A`, `S6-B`, `S6-C`, `S6-D`, `S6-E` as stable lanes
- `R1`, `R2` as the two main directions inside each lane
- `R1a`, `R1b`, `R2a`, `R2b` as substudies when needed

## S6-A Environmental Sentinel Systems

### Role

Monitoring, environmental interpretation, exposure signals, and public-interest environmental intelligence.

### `S6-A-R1` Urban Air, Exposure, and Environmental Risk Signals

#### Substudies

- `S6-A-R1a` pollutant and exposure scoring logic
- `S6-A-R1b` temporal and location-based risk interpretation

#### Why this stays central

This is the clearest public-facing scientific identity for `K-EcoLOGIC Lab`.

### `S6-A-R2` Polluters, Permits, and Oversight Signals

#### Substudies

- `S6-A-R2a` entity-level oversight and permit tracking
- `S6-A-R2b` alert and flagging logic for public accountability

#### Why this matters

This gives `S6-A` a durable civic and scientific use case.

## Operational interpretation

### What is already useful now

Even before deep repo expansion, `S6` can become useful as:

- an environmental sentinel system
- an ecological risk and response interpretation layer
- a biodiversity and climate-signal framework

### What should not happen

Do not let `S6` drift into:

- generic civic dashboards with no science layer
- plant-specific biology that belongs in `S2` or `S3`
- pure engineering products with no ecological question

Those should be mirrored into `TECHNOLOGY` only if the reusable system becomes primary.

## Recommended order inside S6

Best order of execution:

1. `S6-A`
2. `S6-D`
3. `S6-C`
4. `S6-B`
5. `S6-E`

Reason:

- `S6-A` and `S6-D` are the most operational and publicly legible
- `S6-C` strengthens ecological status interpretation
- `S6-B` adds microbiome depth without redefining the sphere
- `S6-E` becomes stronger once the rest of the monitoring logic is already stable
