# S6 K-EcoLOGIC Positioning

## Main conclusion

`K-EcoLOGIC Lab` should not be treated as an environmental microbiome lane.

Based on the current repo content and public app structure, it is much better positioned as:

- `S6-A` Environmental Sentinel Systems

with:

- `K-EcoLOGIC Lab` as the public-facing program label

## Why this is the right fit

The current active modules are:

- `SortSmart Ukraine`
- `Packaging & Sorting Guide`
- `Environmental Briefs`
- `Sorting Assistant`
- `Activist Requests`
- `Air & Exposure`
- `Water Watch`
- `Polluters & Permits`
- `Radiation & Risk`

This is not a microbiome-first research unit.

This is a system for:

- environmental monitoring
- civic and public-interest environmental intelligence
- exposure and risk interpretation
- waste and sorting decision support
- environmental oversight and permit tracking
- dashboard and assistant-style delivery

That is why the strongest scientific home is:

- `S6-A` Environmental Sentinel Systems

and not:

- `S6-B` Environmental Microbiomes

## Recommended interpretation

### Scientific home

`S6-A` = Environmental Sentinel Systems

### Public label

`K-EcoLOGIC Lab`

### Pattern

`S + T`

Why:

- `S` because the domain object is environmental systems, environmental exposure, and ecological risk
- `T` because the actual delivery layer includes app logic, module routing, sorting assistant behavior, data engineering, and public-facing tooling

## What this means structurally

Keep the lettered science map clean:

- `S6-A` Environmental Sentinel Systems
- `S6-B` Environmental Microbiomes
- `S6-C` Biodiversity & Ecosystem Signals
- `S6-D` Pollution, Stress & Response Networks
- `S6-E` Climate-Linked Ecological Change

Then treat:

- `K-EcoLOGIC Lab`

as the branded execution layer living inside:

- `S6-A`

## Recommended research mapping for the existing S6-A programs

### `S6-A-R1` SortSmart Ukraine

Best interpreted as:

- consumer waste sorting logic
- circularity education
- assistant-style environmental decision support

### `S6-A-R2` Air & Exposure Intelligence

Best interpreted as:

- exposure awareness
- local environmental health interpretation
- public-interest environmental monitoring

### `S6-A-R3` Water Watch Ukraine

Best interpreted as:

- water-risk monitoring
- water quality interpretation
- environmental sentinel reporting

### `S6-A-R4` Polluters, Permits, and Environmental Oversight

Best interpreted as:

- regulatory intelligence
- public oversight support
- structured environmental accountability tracking

### `S6-A-R5` Radiation and Environmental Risk

Best interpreted as:

- radiation-linked environmental risk interpretation
- public communication and situational monitoring

## Recommended next research cases

If the goal is to make `S6` useful both scientifically and publicly, the strongest first two research cases are:

1. `S6-A-R2` Air & Exposure Intelligence
2. `S6-A-R4` Polluters, Permits, and Environmental Oversight

## Why these two first

### Air & Exposure Intelligence

- directly useful to people
- scientifically interpretable
- easy to connect with public datasets
- good bridge between environmental science and risk communication

### Polluters, Permits, and Environmental Oversight

- high civic value
- strong watchdog logic
- naturally suited to a structured lab + app model
- good long-term value for dashboards and public-interest tooling

## What should not be done

Do not split `K-EcoLOGIC Lab` into a completely new sphere.

That would make the whole science architecture noisier, not clearer.

It already has a valid home:

- `S6-A` in `SPHERE-I-SCIENCE`

## Practical rule

If the work is about:

- environmental monitoring
- exposure tracking
- oversight
- public environmental signals
- dashboard and assistant delivery

put it in:

- `S6-A`

If the work is primarily about:

- environmental microbial communities
- microbiome ecology
- sequencing-based environmental biology

put it in:

- `S6-B`
